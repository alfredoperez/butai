# Outro — Studio Dark

An end card: a generic wordmark, a two-line CTA with one accent tagline, and a
follow ask. Generic brand only (example.com), no logo, no brand character, no
channel name, no personal copy.

## The Frame

- **The wordmark leads,** display and quiet; the tagline delivers the single
  accent payoff directly beneath it.
- **A mono chip** carries the install / next-step command; the follow ask is a
  soft body line at the bottom.

## Typography

- **Display** for the wordmark and tagline.
- **Mono** for the CTA chip.
- **Body** for the follow ask.

## Motion feel

- The elements rise and settle top to bottom, staggered, on firm eases. No
  bounce, no overshoot.
- Respect reduced-motion: the resting card is fully legible with motion off.

## Do

- Keep the CTA to one command and the ask to one line.
- Keep the brand generic; this is a seed, not a real end card.

## Don't

- No real brand, logo, character, or channel name. No two accent colors.
- No bouncy motion. No em dashes in on-screen copy.
